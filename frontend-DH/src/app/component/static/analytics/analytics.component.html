NDHS MAP READING GUIDE


The National Digital Health Strategy dashboard has two analogous sections - digital health (DH) landscape, and Health & IT landscape. Both sections have the same presentation, and the context is switched at the top:




Currently selected country is shown at the grey button near the top, and the same button features the dropdown selection menu. The Geographical position of all the countries is shown in the map at the central position.

Left panel group shows indicators of current development, while the right section of panels shows indicators of future development. Each panel shows the aggregated score, and two constituent scores just below it.

Clicking either left or right “Show data” button brings up the overview of indicators used in compiling the dashboards. Each section is divided into dichotomous questions formulated so that “Yes” represents a positive outcome.



Each question can also have a “missing” state, which can be interpreted as negative in some dashboard contexts.
